Our services

NEW PICK UP is known for its expertise, its quality of service and its extensive fleet network adapted to your personal needs. Mindful about the environment and our CO2 footprint, we have launched an electric fleet for further choice. Our professional network allows us to answer positively to any transport request such as coach, prestige fleet (Rolls Royce, SUV, limousine, sport cars, as well as armored vehicles), helicopters and private jet.

Airport Transfers

We specialize in airport transfers, providing seamless pick-up and drop-off services at major airports in Paris and other European cities. Our chauffeurs will monitor your flight status, ensuring that they are punctual and ready to assist you with your luggages.

Point-to-Point Transfers

Whether you need transportation between hotels, business meetings, tourist attractions, or any other destination, our private drivers are available for point-to-point transfers. They will take the most efficient routes and ensure that you arrive at your destination on time.

Sightseeing and Tours

If you are looking to explore the sights and attractions of Paris or other European cities, our private drivers can provide personalized sightseeing tours. They are familiar with the local landmarks and can recommend popular destinations or customize an itinerary based on your preferences.

Taylored Transportation Service

NEW PICK UP focuses on providing exclusive transportation solutions for high-profile individuals, celebrities, executives, and other influential personalities attending public or private events. Whether it's a red-carpet event, a corporate gathering, a music festival, a sports tournament, or any other occasion of significance, VIP transportation ensures the smooth and secure movement of these individuals
